01 — About

Vesper opened with one idea: that a menu with no choices could still feel like it was made for you. Every table gets the same six courses, timed the same way, so the kitchen can put its full attention into each one.

Our chefs work from a short list of local producers and let what arrives that morning decide what changes on the menu. Nothing is frozen, nothing is held over, and the menu you eat on a Tuesday won't be the one you'd eat on a Saturday two weeks later.
